Chicken

Chicken meat is a versatile ingredient that can be used in a wide variety of dishes, including soups, stews, salads, sandwiches, and more. It can be seasoned with a variety of spices and herbs to create different flavor profiles.

Beef

Beef is a good source of protein, iron, zinc, and vitamin B12. It can be prepared in many different ways, from grilling and frying to slow cooking and braising. Depending on the cut and preparation method, beef can have a range of flavors and textures.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice, often mixed with sugar and salt, combined with raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and/or other ingredients. The ingredients are then wrapped in seaweed (nori) or served as bite-size pieces.
